WEDNESDAY BIBLE STUDY

True Friends And False Friends

Galatians 4:16-20

INTRODUCTION

  1. Friends can be a wonderful blessing...
    • A source of comfort in times good and bad - Pro 17:17
    • A source of good counsel - Pro 27:9
  2. It is not always easy to know who your true friends are...
    • Sometimes a true friend tells you what you don’t want to hear.
    • Sometimes a false friend tells you what you do want to hear.
  3. In writing to the Galatians, Paul found it necessary to remind them of that truth...
    • As he defended the gospel of justification by faith in Christ
    • As he battled against the influence of those who sought to bind the Law of Moses
In the course of reasoning with the Galatians on sentimental grounds, Paul has some things to say about “True Friends and False Friends” (Ga 4:16-20).

I. TRUE FRIENDS SAY WHAT YOU NEED TO HEAR

A. THEY WILL TELL YOU THE TRUTH

  1. Even if it makes them your enemy - Ga 4:16
  2. Even when the tone hurts - Ga 4:20

B. YOU NEED TRUE FRIENDS

  1. When hearing the gospel of Christ
    • Who will tell you the full gospel - Mk 16:15-16
    • Not just what appeals to our sensitivities - Ga 1:8-9
  2. When growing in grace
    • Who will tell us the need to grow - 2 Pe 3:17-18
    • Not just tickling our ears - 2 Ti 4:3-4
  3. When drifting from God
    • As Paul reached out to the Galatians - Ga 4:19-20
    • As Paul encouraged them to do the same - Ga 6:1

C. HEED THE WISDOM OF DAVID AND SOLOMON

  • Psa 141:5a
  • Pro 13:18
  • Pro 15:31-32
  • Pro 25:12

II. FALSE FRIENDS SAY WHAT THEY WANT YOU TO HEAR

A. THEY WILL TELL YOU WHAT THEY WANT

  1. When it makes you on their side - Ga 4:17
  2. When you have something they want - Ga 6:12-13

B. YOU NEED TO BEWARE OF FALSE FRIENDS

  1. When you are being courted with zeal
    • Their motives may not be right - Ro 16:17-18
    • Their knowledge may be wrong - Ro 10:1-2
  2. When you are being manipulated
    • As false teachers manipulated the Galatians - Ga 4:17
    • A tactic first used by Satan - 2 Co 11:3
  3. When you are pursued only in their presence
    • Paul sought their devotion even in absence - Ph 1:27
    • False friends forget you when absent

C. HEED THE WISDOM OF SOLOMON

“Faithful are the wounds of a friend, but the kisses of an enemy are deceitful.” - Proverbs 27:6

CONCLUSION

  1. Many people have been led astray by the wrong kind of friends...
    • Good habits corrupted by bad company - 1 Co 15:33
    • Immature Christians deceived by false teachers - Ep 4:14
  2. May we learn from Paul and the Galatians...
    • True friends say what you need to hear
    • False friends say what they want you to hear

Speaking of true friends, the greatest friend we can have is Jesus.

John 15:13-14
