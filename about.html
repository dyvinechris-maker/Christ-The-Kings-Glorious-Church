PASTORATES


Founder

Pastor Samuel Omojola

Pastor S.O. Omojola was the founder of Christ The King’s Glorious Church, a Bible-believing ministry known for its emphasis on holiness, righteousness, and truth. The church was officially registered in 2003 and continues to operate under the leadership of his successors.

General Overseer

Pastor Wale Omojola

Pastor Adewale Omojola is a leading minister at Christ The King’s Glorious Church in Lagos, Nigeria, continuing the legacy of the late Pastor S.O. Omojola. He is known for his prophetic declarations, Bible-based teachings, and leadership within the church.

Pastor Abiodun Ayodeji

Senior Pastor

Pastor Abiodun Ayodeji has stood with Christ The King's Glorious Church for over 20 years with a heart for Prayer & Evangelism.

Pastor Mrs. Tope Omojola

Co-Pastor

Pastor Mrs. Tope Omojola leads the Women's Ministry and oversees the counseling department.

Pastor Joseph Gbonjubola

Co-Pastor

Passionate about raising the next generation of believers through sound teaching, spiritual mentorship, and a strong foundation in Christ.
